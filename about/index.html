I am Hosna Sattar. I am originally from Iran, Mashhad. I moved to Germany in 2011 to start my master studies in visual computing at Saarland University, and then I in 2015, decided to start my PhD at Max-Planck institute for computer science at Saarland University.

The focus of my PhD thesis was on search intent prediction from implicit human cues which for this aim I used human gaze and body shape. Since March 2020 I am working as an applied scientist at Zalando, Berlin. .
